My journey

Becoming a web developer has been an exciting journey! I began learning html, css & javascript about six months ago. At the time I was still working at my dead end job, attempting to squeeze in precious hours for coding. Once I realized I'd rather be coding than to be at work, doing something I did not see myself doing for the rest of my life, I was determined to find a way.

My fiance and I received a huge break when she found out she was accepted into the nursing program at UTA in Arlington. We decided to take a chance, leave everything behind in Houston, and follow our dreams. Currently, I work day in and day out, doing something I fell in love with: coding.

I love to learn, thank God, because there is so much to learn! For me, coding is twice as rewarding as it is a struggle. Being able to bring a flat image design to life, with full functionality, brings with it the most satisfying feeling. The problem solving aspect is something that I do not shy away from, and when I finally solve an error, well, there's just no better feeling. I was born for this!
